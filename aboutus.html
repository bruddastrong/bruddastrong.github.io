David Choi

Hey, whats good y’all! I’m David and I’ve been constantly active starting from middle school, in which I was part of the Track and Field team pursuing Pole Vault. It’s always a passion for me to break my limits at whatever activity I am involved in. Now that I am in college, I am involved in BruddaStrong to improve my physical body performance and strength.
