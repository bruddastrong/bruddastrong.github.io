Who are we?

Hey there! We're BruddaStrong and we're proud to be releasing this website as a way of showing whoever's interested our personal tips and tricks when it comes to food, fitness, and health!

We will also be using this website as a way to track our personal fitness progress and look forward to seeing how we improve as the years go by. Remember, fitness is a long-term lifestyle and results do not come quickly! Anyway, thanks for following us on our journey and we hope we can help you along yours!

Motivation

As busy college students at the University of Michigan, we frequently see how heavy courseloads and stacked extracurricular commitments can lead to unhealthy lifestyles, whether it's due to lack of exercise, poor diet, sleep deprivation, etc.

We have all been through these struggles and that's why we decided to create BruddaStrong - by being the best versions of ourselves and constantly striving for improvement we hope to not only improve our health and lifestyles but also to influence others around us.

We all have our separate gym goals and desires but what we have in common is our drive to improve and better ourselves no matter what it takes.
